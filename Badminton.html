About Badminton

Badminton is a fast-paced racket sport where players hit a shuttlecock over a net, aiming to land it in the opponent's court, played as singles (1v1) or doubles (2v2). It's played indoors professionally on a rectangular court with specific lines, using lightweight rackets and a shuttlecock (birdie). Games use a 21-point rally scoring system, requiring a two-point lead to win a game, with the first to two games winning the match.
