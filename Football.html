About Football

Football (or soccer) is the world's most popular sport, a team game where two teams of 11 players try to score goals by kicking or heading a ball into the opponent's net, using feet, head, and chest but not hands (except for the goalkeeper). Key aspects include teamwork, dribbling, passing, and shooting, with major tournaments like the FIFA World Cup uniting fans globally, featuring stars like Lionel Messi and Cristiano Ronaldo, and Sunil Chhetri evolving with technology like VAR.
